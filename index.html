About Drishti 2025

Drishti is an annual international conference that brings together scholars, innovators, and professionals in the fields of Management and Technology. The 2025 edition aims to build on past successes and provide a global platform for new ideas and research.

Objectives

  • Promote interdisciplinary research in management and technology
  • Encourage young minds to present innovative ideas
  • Facilitate collaboration between academia and industry
  • Highlight emerging trends and practices
